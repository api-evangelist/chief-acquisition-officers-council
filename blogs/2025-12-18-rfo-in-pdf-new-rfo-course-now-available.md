RFO in PDF & New RFO Course Now Available Anonymous (not verified) Thu, 12/18/2025 - 10:00 The Federal Acquisition Regulatory Council has released a full PDF of all updated model deviations as part of the ongoing Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) Initiative, in accordance with Executive Order 14275 and OMB Memorandum M-25-26 . The RFO in PDF , which includes all model deviations as well as the consolidated parts 2 and 52, is being provided based on feedback to support ease of searching content within the RFO. A new RFO course, Navigating the FAR Framework , has also been posted this week.
