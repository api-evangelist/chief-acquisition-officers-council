Modernizing Acquisition.gov: RFO Page Redesign & Enhancements Anonymous (not verified) Fri, 05/15/2026 - 10:00 On April 30, 2026, Acquisition.gov launched a refreshed website design, featuring a redesign of the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) pages. While the regulatory content itself remains exactly the same, this update introduces a modern "look and feel" designed to better serve the acquisition workforce and industry partners. Key improvements include: Streamlined Navigation: Finding critical information is now faster and more intuitive. Mobile Responsiveness: A seamless experience across all devices, from desktops to smartphones. Enhanced Accessibility: Improved standards to ensure the platform is inclusive for all users. This redesign is a major milestone in transforming Acquisition.gov into a more dynamic, user-centric hub for the federal contracting community. Note : The Acquisition.gov search engine does not support RFO page queries. It is recommended to use the RFO PDF version's Ctrl + F function for accurate content searching.
