FAR Overhaul Model Deviation Text Update Anonymous (not verified) Tue, 03/17/2026 - 11:00 Today, March 16th, 2026, the Federal Acquisition Regulatory Council has updated the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) to conform with the changes to the codified Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) as outlined in FAR Case 2025-007, Trade Agreements Thresholds, Final Rule , effective March 13, 2026. The threshold changes were updated in the following RFO parts: RFO Part 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System RFO Part 5 - Publicizing Contract Actions RFO Part 10 - Market Research RFO Part 22 - Application of Labor Laws to Government Acquisitions RFO Part 25 - Foreign Acquisition RFO Part 52 - Solicitation Provisions and Contract Clauses To view all available rewritten FAR Parts and agency deviations, visit: https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul/far-part-deviation-guide . Stay informed. View the latest FAR Overhaul news and announcements at: https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul . &&
